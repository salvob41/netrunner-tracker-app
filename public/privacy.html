Trackster Taka — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-09

Trackster Taka - Netrunner ("the app") is an unofficial offline companion for Netrunner — a digital tracker for credits, clicks, tags, agenda points, core damage, bad publicity, MU, hand size, and link. Same idea as the Magic: the Gathering life-tracker web apps that have circulated for years, only purpose-built for the Netrunner LCG.

What we collect

Nothing.

  • The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.
  • No accounts, sign-ups, or logins.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, telemetry, or crash reporting services.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party SDKs that process personal information.

Permissions the app requests

  • Vibration — used for haptic feedback on score and click changes.
  • Prevent device from sleeping — keeps the screen on during a match.

Neither permission is used to access, derive, or transmit personal data.

Network usage

The app makes no network requests during normal operation. It runs entirely on-device and is fully functional offline.

Data storage

Match state lives only in the device's working memory while the app is open. Closing the app discards it. Nothing is persisted to disk.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data of any kind, it complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, and equivalent regulations by default. Suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If future versions of the app introduce any data handling, this policy will be updated before those features ship, and the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
